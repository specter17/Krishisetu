Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get weather alerts for my area?

You can subscribe to weather alerts by selecting your district on the Weather page and clicking the "Subscribe to Alerts" button. You'll need to provide your mobile number to receive SMS alerts.

How often are the market prices updated?

Our market prices are updated daily from official mandi sources. During peak harvest seasons, some prices may be updated multiple times a day.

Is there a cost to use Krishsetu services?

All basic services on Krishsetu are completely free for farmers. Some premium services like personalized expert consultations may have nominal charges.

Can I get information in my regional language?

Yes, Krishsetu is available in Hindi, Punjabi, and English. You can change the language from the settings menu at the top of the page.

How can I report incorrect information on the website?

Please use the "Report Error" option available on each information card or contact our support team with details of the incorrect information.
